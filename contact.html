Data Science

What is data?

Data is everywhere. Everything you do, you leave behind digital trace or data. Everything you buy, download, see, anything that is happening on a digital machine will eventually convert to data. Your bank account contains data about how much you are spending, your credit score, social security number, and amount of money that you are saving.

According to the Northeastern University article, the total amount of data in the world is 4.4 zettabytes in 2013, and researchers are predicting that there will be 44 zettabytes by 2020. If that doesn't sound crazy to you, let me visually represent what those numbers are. Remember that average computer has arond 1 terabytes of data, this is not including small devices. Now, 1 zettabytes will equal 1,000,000,000 terabytes!

What is data science?

Now with this big data, data scientists will use algorithms and mathematics to analyze or solve problems. To see what data science can do or provide, you can visit here. With data science, people invented artificial intelligence such as self-driving cars, facial recognition, predicting next mountain fire, and more. It is being used widely by big corporations and big companies with massive amount of data behind their servers.

Impact of Data Science and Future

However..

Currently, data science is being used only by big corporations and companies to generate more revenue and profits. They hire both data scientists and business intelligence engineers to create insights and stories within the big data. Facebook, as an example, use machine learning or artificial intelligence to calculate what kind of advertisements will be shown to each individual users to generate maximum revenue as possible. Similar to other big companies, they are monitoring every users' data and increase their profits and revenues using A.I.

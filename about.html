INTRODUCTION

Welcome to Sybest and thank you for using our services. To introduce what Sybest is, Sybest will like to talk about data science. Data science is a tool that analyze big data and create insights or stories within that data. Without data, data science wouldn't exist in the first place. Currently in Silicon Valley, big companies like Apple, Facebook, and Google are hiring data scientists to make predictions, insights, and patterns using big data. For example, Facebook monitors your behavior by collecting data of your actions on Facebook application and predict which type of advertisements you will be interested in. Thus, Facebook can bring more revenue on their side just by analyizing you.

What is Sybest about?

Currently, only big corporations use data science to increase profit and revenue. Data science is also known as artificial intelligence because it can predict future using data.

Sybest has a main business goal.
